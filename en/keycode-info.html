About Keycode Info

Keyboard Keycode Capturer. Press any key on the keyboard to show its event.keyCode, event.key, and event.code in the center of the screen. This page is designed as a lightweight, static, browser-friendly utility that opens quickly and keeps the core workflow focused.

Use Keycode Info for everyday development, data cleanup, formatting, conversion, validation, or productivity tasks without installing extra desktop software. Whenever the tool processes input, it is intended to run locally in the browser as much as possible, helping reduce unnecessary uploads and keeping the experience fast.
