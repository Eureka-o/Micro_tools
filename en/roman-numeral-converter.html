Rule Hint

  • Supports up to 3999, i.e. Roman numeral MMMCMXCIX.
  • Supports standard subtractive notation, such as IV, IX, XL, XC, CD, and CM.
  • Non-standard forms such as IIII, VV, IC, and MMMM will be treated as invalid input.

About Roman Numeral Converter

Roman numeral and Arabic numeral conversion tool. Supports Roman numerals up to 3999 and includes error prompts for invalid input. This page is designed as a lightweight, static, browser-friendly utility that opens quickly and keeps the core workflow focused.

Use Roman Numeral Converter for everyday development, data cleanup, formatting, conversion, validation, or productivity tasks without installing extra desktop software. Whenever the tool processes input, it is intended to run locally in the browser as much as possible, helping reduce unnecessary uploads and keeping the experience fast.
